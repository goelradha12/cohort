Audio/video Type Description
Audio MP3 Popular for music and online streaming due to its compressed file size and decent quality.
WAV (Waveform Audio File Format) Uncompressed and high-quality, commonly used in professional audio production.
AAC (Advanced Audio Codec) Used in Apple devices and YouTube, offering better sound quality than MP3 at similar bit rates.
Video MP4 The most widely used format for videos due to its high compatibility and efficient compression.
AVI (Audio Video Interleave) An older format developed by Microsoft, known for large file sizes and broad compatibility.
MKV (Matroska Video Format) Supports multiple audio and subtitle tracks, commonly used for high-quality video storage.
